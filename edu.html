Academic Catalog

Welcome to the Eli Daniels University Academic Catalog

Eli Daniels University provides a world-class education to students who desire a unique approach to academic study. With ten schools and numerous constituent divisions, EDU offers undergraduate and graduate courses that are intellectually demanding and practical to help students reach their academic and career goals.

This academic catalog will provide information on school, divisional, and course policies, course requirements, and any other relevant information that is pertinent to the academic success of a student at EDU.

Currently, the catalog only contains information for Bachelor's degrees with iterative updates expected to include schools beyond Engineering and Applied Science and Liberal Arts as well as graduate programs for all schools.
