Division of Engineering

Domestic Applicants:

  • All divisions and courses require the science pathway.
  • Successful applicants typically have a 4.0 average or above with 5s in Mathematics C and Science A.

US Applicants:

  • Completion of a high school diploma
    • College prep subjects to include the following
      • Mathematics through Calculus and/or Statistics
      • Biology, Chemistry, and Physics
      • Two years of History
      • Four years of English
      • Some experience in foreign language, arts, and social sciences
  • AP Calculus and two of the following: AP Chemistry, AP Physics, AP Biology
  • Successful applicants typically have a 3.5 unweighted GPA or above, have taken all four recommended APs, and have taken a diverse and rigorous program

IB Diploma Applicants*:

  • Completion of the IB Diploma Programme
  • HL Mathematics and two HL Sciences.
  • Successful applicants typically have a diverse record of qualifications and have taken advantage of the educational opportunities that the IB Diploma Programme provides.

Canadian Applicants (excluding Quebec):

  • Completion of a high school diploma
  • Year 11/12 elections should include topics in calculus, and two of the following: chemistry, physics, and biology.
  • High school subjects should also include four years of English, two years of History, and experience in foreign language, arts, and social sciences.
  • Successful applicants typically have a high average and have taken a diverse and rigorous program beyond the required subjects

Quebecois Applicants*:

  • Completion of CEGEP diploma or English-language equivalent
  • Must include two terms of Calculus and Physics with at least one term of chemistry. An additional term of chemistry or a term of biology is also required.
  • There is no specific preferred field of study as long as the pre-requisites are fulfilled. Successful applicants will have a high average, regardless of field of study, and have taken a rigorous program beyond minimum expectations for the diploma.
  • Documents such as transcripts should be submitted with English translations in addition to the original French.

UK Applicants:

  • Completion of A-levels in Mathematics and two of the following: Chemistry, Physics, Biology
  • All four are highly recommended
  • Successful applicants will typically have earned A*/A/A and participated in activities beyond STEM.

All other applicants*:

  • Please submit your high school record or your country’s equivalent pre-university qualifications.
  • Documents must be submitted with English translations in addition to in their original language.

*Those who have had fewer than four years of education in an English-language school from Age 12+ will be required to submit proof of English competence through high-level performance on IELTS.
